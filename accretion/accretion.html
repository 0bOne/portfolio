Accretion Simulator Instructions

This interactive simulator models gravitational attraction and mass accretion between thousands of particles. Here's how to use it:

Controls

  • Particles: Set the initial number of particles (default: 5000).
  • Zoom: Adjust the current zoom level (1 = normal scale).

Buttons

  • Start: Begin the simulation.
  • Stop: Pause the simulation.
  • Largest: Recenter the camera on the largest particle (most mass).
  • Average: Recenter the camera on the average center of mass.
  • Zoom In / Zoom Out: Change the zoom level to inspect detail or view the full system.

Simulation Behavior

  • Each particle has random position, velocity, and mass.
  • Gravitational forces are applied between all particles.
  • When particles collide, they merge. The larger absorbs the smaller, conserving momentum.
  • The simulation runs in real-time. Performance may vary based on particle count and browser.
